Our Approach

ABCO Process Consultancy is a specialized engineering consultancy, providing comprehensive services for petroleum oil processing plants. Our expertise spans a full range of technical services, from project inception through commissioning, with a focus on process engineering, specialized studies, and analyses.

Our Strength

Our core strength lies in our expertise in process design, fabrication, erection, and commissioning for all refinery and distillation units. We also provide meticulously crafted plant layouts and engineering documentation to ensure seamless government approvals.

Our Team

Our team is comprised of highly skilled process engineering professionals who bring both experience and commitment to every project. Their dedication to quality, timeliness, and budget adherence allows us to consistently deliver superior results.
