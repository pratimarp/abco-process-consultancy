Our Expertise

ABCO Process Consultancy offers a wide range of services across various industries globally, with a strong focus on Oil & Energy, Petrochemicals, and Waste Processing and Recycling.

Process Engineering

Our Process Engineers provide end-to-end solutions, covering the design, construction, commissioning, operation, and decommissioning of process plants. Specializing in distillation plants for petroleum and petrochemical products, blending plants, waste recycling facilities, and storage terminals, we offer comprehensive services such as:

  • Complete process design and engineering (basic and detailed)
  • Troubleshooting and optimization of existing systems
  • Feasibility studies and process simulations
  • Project management and startup support
  • Compliance with engineering standards (ASME, DIN, API)
  • Plant equipment specification, selection, and procurement
  • Process control and instrumentation design
  • Production planning and quality control
  • Plant modifications and improvements

Plant Performance Evaluation and Troubleshooting

Our assessment services help improve your plant's operational efficiency, productivity, and reliability. We focus on enhancing energy efficiency, safety, and workforce well-being, while also reducing lifecycle costs. Our expertise includes:

  • Evaluating plant performance and identifying areas for improvement
  • Implementing operational and maintenance strategies to optimize productivity
  • Ensuring compliance with safety and environmental regulations
  • Lowering energy consumption and increasing overall efficiency

Environmental Solutions

ABCO's team of expert environmental engineers is dedicated to providing innovative and sustainable solutions in line with the latest environmental regulations. We offer services such as:

  • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)
  • Risk Assessments (Qualitative and Quantitative)
  • Waste Management and Pollution Control (Air, Noise, Water)
  • Occupational Health and Safety Services
  • Environmental Modelling
  • Assistance with regulatory approvals from governmental bodies

We help industries achieve their environmental goals by focusing on sustainability—Recycle, Reuse, and Reduce—and ensure compliance with local regulations to minimize environmental impact.
