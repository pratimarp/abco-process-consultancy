About ABCO Process Consultancy

ABCO Process Consultancy is a leading Engineering Consultancy Company providing comprehensive services in the Oil & Gas, Energy, and Process industries. Our expertise spans oil refining/distillation, petrochemical plants, specialty chemicals, blending plants, waste recycling facilities, and related process/manufacturing units.

Core Services

We specialize in delivering a full range of technical and consultancy services from project inception to commissioning. These include:

  • Process Engineering
  • Process Equipment Design and Piping Design
  • Specialized Engineering Studies & Analyses
  • Engineering Drawings and Plant Layouts
  • Production Planning, Quality Control, and Process Optimization
  • Technical Feasibility Studies and Debottlenecking
  • Troubleshooting for Process Units

Geographical Reach

Our projects span across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, catering to a diverse clientele that includes independent Oil & Energy companies, private investors, and governmental institutions. With a deep understanding of regional needs and global industry standards, we deliver tailored solutions that address the unique challenges of each project. Our extensive expertise enables us to contribute to the sustainable growth and development of these regions' energy and process industries, fostering long-term partnerships built on trust and exceptional results.

Expertise and Strengths

Comprehensive capabilities in process design, fabrication, erection, and commissioning of refinery and distillation plants. Proven expertise in optimizing and troubleshooting facilities such as petrochemical plants, specialty chemicals units, and waste recycling facilities. Experience in preparing engineering documents and plant layouts to facilitate various governmental approvals.

Commitment to Excellence

Our team of highly experienced professionals ensures project execution that meets the highest standards of quality, timeliness, and cost-efficiency. We have built strong, enduring relationships with our clients through our unwavering commitment to their needs.

ABCO Process Consultancy is recognized for its passion for innovation, exceptional consulting services, and delivering lasting results. We continuously strive to uphold and enhance this reputation by maintaining the highest standards of service.
